Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can students from other colleges participate?
Yes, it's open to all college students.
Q: Is there any registration fee?
Yes, ₹1000 per team or ₹400 per head for outside participants. Free for Thapar students.
Q: Will participation certificates be provided?
Yes, to all valid participants.
Q: How do I register for multiple events?
You can select multiple events on the registration form.
Q: Are team events allowed?
Yes, some events are team-based. Check event details.
Q: What is the venue for Saturnalia 2025?
All events are inside Thapar Campus. Follow our Instagram for updates.
Q: Whom can I contact for more information?
Contact via Instagram or email at saturnalia@thapar.edu.
