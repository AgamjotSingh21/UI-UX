Welcome to Saturnalia

Dive into the most exhilarating inter-college festival of the year — Saturnalia 2025! From November 24th to 26th, join us for three unforgettable days packed with thrilling competitions, innovative workshops, and spectacular cultural performances. Whether you're a coder ready to conquer the hackathon, a dancer eager to own the stage, or simply a festival enthusiast looking to soak in the electrifying atmosphere, Saturnalia offers something for everyone. Experience intense rap battles, cutting-edge blockchain bootcamps, and high-energy dance-offs alongside brilliant minds and passionate artists from colleges across the region. Come be part of a vibrant community, unleash your talents, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Get ready to build, battle, and vibe — because Saturnalia 2025 is where legends are made!
